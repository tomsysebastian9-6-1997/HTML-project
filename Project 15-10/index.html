Deen Dayal Upadhayaya Grammen Kaushalya Yojana

A Grammen Kaushalya Yojana The Ministry of Rural Devolopment(MoRD) announced the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana(DDU-GKY) on Antyodaya Diswas-25th September 2014. DDU_GKY is a part of the Nnational Rural Livelihood Mission(NRLM). tasked with the dual objectives of adding diversity to the incomes of rural poor families and cater to the carrer aspirations of rural youth.
